About Me

Software Developer

  • Age :

  • Education: Msc. Computer and Information Science, University of Ljubljana

  • Mail : anze.medved@outlook.com

My hometown is Kranj, a beautiful town in the north-western part of Slovenia. I attended a local high school, Gimnazija Kranj, which I finished with distinction in 2012. The same year I enrolled in an undergraduate university study programme at the Faculty of Computer and Information Science, which is part of the University of Ljubljana. I graduated in 2015 and received Dean's commendation for exemplary academic performance in the undergraduate programme. I continued my studies at the same faculty with the Masters programme, which I finished in 2019.

My field of interest is very broad. Most of my subjects at faculty covered machine learning, artificial intelligence and computer vision (some of my seminar works are linked in my works section). On the other hand, my professional work focused more on backend software development in Java, database management with Oracle and Postgres databases, DevOps, etc. I applied my skills to different fields, including demanding warehouse logistics and satellite data processing. In my free time I try to follow recent trends and state-of-the-art technologies to broaden my knowledge and learn something new.

I always tend to find some time for other activities such as hiking in the beautiful mountains of Slovenia, cycling through country roads or just simply taking a walk in nature.

Feel free to contact me, I am always looking to learn something new and improve my existing knowledge.
